在击杀凋灵时手持下界之星，它会变成 Totem of Resurrection —— 一种加强版的、“压缩型”不死图腾（Totem of Undying）。
在击杀凋灵时手持 Totem of Resurrection ，即可吸收凋灵的灵魂来为自己所用。手持 Totem of Resurrection 时按下 TAB 键，屏幕上方玩家名字右侧的黄色数字即为玩家已经吸收的灵魂数量。只要灵魂数目不小于 1 ， Totem of Resurrection 因玩家死亡而激活时不会像寻常的不死图腾那样消失，而是会消耗 1 点灵魂来复活玩家。耗尽灵魂后再次激活图腾会使其退化为下界之星。
Totem of Resurrection 的效力比原有的不死图腾要强力得多。
不同的情形下，左下角对话框中会出现字体颜色不同的提示：图腾生成（紫色）、图腾激活（黄色）、灵魂吸收（蓝色）、灵魂耗尽（金色）、图腾退化（红色）。
附注：
1.玩家可以同时拥有多个 Totem of Resurrection ；
2.图腾生成时是按组转化，因此注意不要在生成图腾时手持多个下界之星；
3.吸收的灵魂寄存于玩家精神之内，不依赖于具体的图腾，也不会因玩家死亡而消散；
4.图腾生成的优先级高于灵魂吸收。

Hold a Nether Star when killing a Wither, the star will become Totem of Resurrection - a enhanced, "compressed" version of Totem of Undying.
Hold a Totem of Resurrection when killing a Wither to absort its soul for player's benefit. Hold a Totem of Resurrection, press TAB, the number on the right of the player name on top of the screen will show how many souls the player has absorbed. As long as the number is no less than 1, Totem of Resurrection will not disappear just like Totem of Undying when activated, it instead cosumes 1 point of soul to respawn the player. Activating the totem when souls are used up will make it degrade to Nether Star.
The power of Totem of Resurrection is much more effective than vanilla totem.
Under different circumstances, there are notifications in the chatbox showing in varied colors: totem created (light purple), totem activated (yellow), soul absorbed (aqua), souls depleted (gold), totem degraded (red).
PS:
1. A player can have more than one of Totem of Resurrection at the same time;
2. Totem creating process will transform a stack of nether stars, so make sure that not holding several of them;
3. Souls absorbed are in the spirit of the player, neither relying on the specific totem nor disappering when the player dies;
4. The creation of totem is prior to the absorption of souls.
